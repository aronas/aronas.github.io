USE OF ELECTRICITY


A typical desktop computer uses about 65–250 watts. Add another 15–70 watts for an LCD monitor. A typical cable modem uses 7 watts, a router: 4.5 watts.

Most laptop computers use about 15–60 watts, far less than desktops.
